⚠️ IV Pump Recall (Model X2000)

Posted: July 18, 2025 - 09:30 AM

The FDA has issued a Class I recall for Model X2000 IV pumps due to potential flow rate inconsistencies. Immediate removal and substitution required in all units.

⚠️ Medication Labeling Error – Morphine Sulfate

Posted: July 17, 2025 - 3:15 PM

Vials of Morphine Sulfate 10 mg/mL have been found mislabeled as 1 mg/mL. Barcode scanning and second-nurse verification are strongly advised until recall is complete.

⚠️ Infection Control Breach – OR #4

Posted: July 17, 2025 - 08:00 AM

Improper sterilization detected in Operating Room #4. All elective procedures are suspended until full disinfection and inspection are completed.
